Locations

There are many restaurants. Some restaurants are quick and easy and others you stay at for a couple of hours. Some are very expensive but others are affordable. Not all restaurants have waiters or waitresses. Some of them have drive-thrus and others do not. There are three main categories: fast food restaurants, sit-down chain restaurants, and Five Star restaurants. One category of restaurants is fast food. Most of these restaurants have a drive-thru. Not everyone eats inside the restaurant. Some people take it home or even eat it inside their car. When a person orders their food, they can either drive through the drive-thru or go inside to order. A person's order can sometimes take longer if the person goes inside to order rather than going through the drive-thru. A person can get their order the fastest by going to a fast food restaurant. Most fast food restaurants sell a lot of fried and greasy foods, but there are some that also serve healthy foods. There are no waiters or waitresses; there is only the person behind the counter who takes the orders and the person who occasionally wipes down the tables. Burger King, McDonalds, and Popeye's are three examples of fast food restaurants.Another category of restaurants is sit-down chain restaurants. When a group of people walk in the door of one of these restaurants, there will normally be a hostess standing behind a podium waiting to seat them. Then after they have looked at their menu a waiter or waitress will take their order. These restaurants serve all kinds of food, including foods that fast food restaurants would serve. The service in these restaurants is a lot slower than fast food restaurants. Most sit-down chain restaurants have some sort of theme in them. Like a Mexican theme, Wild West theme, or an Australia theme. Some examples of chain restaurants are Outback Steakhouse, Apple Bees, and Red Lobster.
